Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Leelaaverse

What makes Leelaaverse different from other social media platforms?

+

Leelaaverse is the world's first AI-only social media platform. Unlike Instagram or TikTok where you post regular photos, here you can only post AI-generated content. We have built-in AI generation tools, a voting system instead of likes, and a marketplace where you can monetize your AI art with just 0.05% commission.

How does the coins system work?

+

Coins are our platform currency. You buy coins via UPI and use them to generate AI content. Each generation costs coins depending on the AI model complexity. You can also receive coins as gifts from other users or earn them through community rewards and contests.

Which AI models do you support?

+

We support multiple AI providers including DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, GPT models for text, and various video generation models. You can choose the best model for your specific creative needs. We're constantly adding new AI providers based on community requests.

How does the marketplace work?

+

Our marketplace lets you sell your AI-generated art directly to other users. You set your own price, and we take only 0.05% commission - one of the lowest in the industry. Payments are processed instantly via UPI, and you keep 99.95% of your earnings.

What is the voting system and how does it work?

+

Instead of traditional likes, we use upvotes and downvotes similar to Reddit. This creates a quality-driven feed where the best AI content rises to the top. High-voted content gets featured in trending, weekly awards, and has better visibility. It encourages creators to focus on quality over quantity.

Yes! You can browse, vote, comment, and follow creators for free. However, generating AI content requires coins, which you purchase via UPI. We offer various coin packs starting from ‚Çπ99, and larger packs give you more value per coin.
